Digital PID Controller

In this project, my group mates and I implemented a digital PID controller for a turntable speed control system with an analog circuit system. Using a DC motor-driven turntable with a tachometer, we designed and tuned a PID controller to meet specific performance criteria: percent overshoot of less than 10%, settling time less than 500 ms, and rise time less than 200 ms.

During the laboratory we used LabView software created by the group for data acquisition and tuning components, and MATLAB for theoretical simulations.

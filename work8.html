Aluminum Car Project

Worked with machines such as a milling machine to shape a block of aluminum into a small car. Features include wheels, a chamfered front and a rounded back.

  • Used a lathe to create the four circular wheels on the car, including a diameter change in the middle of the wheels for design purposes.
  • Tools used include measurements with a dial caliper and height gauge, and use of a milling machine and lathe.
  • Followed 2D schematic drawings and measurements to meet the correct tolerances for the design.

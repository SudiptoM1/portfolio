NASA L’SPACE MCA PDR

Over the course of the Fall 2024 semester, I’ve participated in the NASA L’SPACE Mission Concept Academy as a thermal engineer, where I demonstrated knowledge by following the NASA mission life cycle, up to submission of a Preliminary Design Review, for a robotic space mission by attending and engaging in weekly lectures taught by NASA scientists, engineers, and managers.

I supported the Thermal system design of a rover to carry out the mission’s science objectives, by researching similar mission’s architecture, performing trade studies between subsystem architectures, and completing CAD design of the subsystem by using Siemens NX.
